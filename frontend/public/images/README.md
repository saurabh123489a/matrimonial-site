# Images Directory

Place your wedding ceremony images here:

## Required Images:

1. **bridal-lehenga-1.jpg** - Red & Gold Lehenga bridal photography (bride lying down with lehenga spread)
   - Used in Hero section and CTA section
   
2. **bridal-lehenga-2.jpg** - Overhead shot of bride in traditional attire (rotated view)
   - Used in Hero section and CTA section
   
3. **bridal-lehenga-3.jpg** - Close-up overhead shot of bride with eyes closed
   - Used in Hero section and CTA section

## Image Specifications:

- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Size**: Landscape orientation (1920x1080 or similar aspect ratio)
- **Quality**: High quality for best display
- **File Size**: Optimize for web (under 500KB recommended per image)

## How to Add Images:

1. Place your image files in this directory (`frontend/public/images/`)
2. Name them exactly as listed above:
   - `bridal-lehenga-1.jpg`
   - `bridal-lehenga-2.jpg`
   - `bridal-lehenga-3.jpg`
3. The images will be automatically available at `/images/filename.jpg`
